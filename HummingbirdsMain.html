Fun With Hummingbirds



You Can Attract Hummingbirds

Many people don't believe they can attract hummingbirds to their yards. However, this is not true. With the right plants and a few feeders you can entice hummingbirds to visit your yard all season long.

You'll need:

Feeders

Hummingbirds will happily visit your feeders if you fill them with nectar. They like a mix of 4 parts table sugar to 1 part tap water. Be sure to boil the water before mixing it with the sugar. This will kill any bacteria present that might make the hummingbirds sick.
